What was the bug:
The error was the index was going beyond the options given in the list 
triggering an ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException.
How i find it:
I use the debugger and set a breakpoint within the loop. I watch the
index as it would go through the list and eventually out of bounds.
What's the fix:
The fix is simple change the i<=alerts.length to i<alerts.length. With 
that the index should stay within the correct bounds.
